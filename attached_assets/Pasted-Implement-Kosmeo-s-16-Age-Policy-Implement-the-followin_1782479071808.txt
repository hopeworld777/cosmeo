Implement Kosmeo's 16+ Age Policy

Implement the following age policy consistently throughout the entire project.

Official Policy

Kosmeo is available only to users aged 16 and older.

Users under 16 may not create an account.
Users aged 16–17 may use Kosmeo only with permission from a parent or legal guardian.
Users 18+ may use the platform normally.

Do not add age verification or ID verification. This is a declaration-based policy.

1. Registration Page

Update the registration form.

Add a required checkbox before the Create Account button.

English:

I confirm that I am at least 16 years old. If I am 16–17 years old, I have permission from my parent or legal guardian to use Kosmeo.

Georgian:

The checkbox must be required before registration.

Use the existing validation and i18n system.
